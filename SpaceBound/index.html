#About us

SpaceBound - More Than Just a Space Journey. We Create Opportunities for Everyone to Redefine Their Limits, Get Inspired by the Incredible Beauty of Space, and Experience the Grandeur of the Universe.

Join us on our journey through the stars and let us help you fulfill your dream of space travel. SpaceBound - Opening New Horizons and Bringing Your Space Dream to Life.

#Tour description

01

Open the Door to a New World

Your space adventure will begin with a total immersion in training and preparation to make you feel comfortable and confident during your flight. You will meet our team of experts who will ensure your safety during your flight.

02

Discover the Magic of Space Travel

During the flight, you will experience incredible excitement as your spaceship shoots up, breaking through the Earth's atmosphere. You will see how the earth's gravity weakens and you freely soar in weightlessness.

03

Return with Eternal Memories

Returning to Earth, you will have a new view of the world. This journey will change you forever. You will feel deep gratitude for our planet and realize its vulnerability. You will carry unforgettable memories of your journey in your heart.
